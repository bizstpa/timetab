TimeTab Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

TimeTab is a browser extension for snoozing tabs. This policy explains what data it handles. The short version: everything stays on your device.

What is stored

When you snooze a tab, TimeTab saves that tab's URL, title, icon, and the scheduled reopen time, along with your theme setting. This information is stored locally on your device using the browser's chrome.storage.local and is used only to reopen your tabs at the times you choose.

What is read

TimeTab reads the active tab's URL, title, and icon only at the moment you click to snooze that tab. It does not read other tabs or your browsing in the background.

What is not done

TimeTab does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data. There are no servers, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. No information ever leaves your device.

Removing your data

You can remove stored data at any time by cancelling snoozes from the extension, or by uninstalling the extension, which clears its local storage.

Contact

Questions can be raised on the project's issue tracker at https://github.com/bizstpa/timetab/issues.
